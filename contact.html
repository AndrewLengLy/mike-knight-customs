After You Submit

What Happens Next

01 Estimate

Free Estimate

We inspect the damage, including what a drive-by estimate can't see, and write the repair plan to the manufacturer's published procedures. You get a plain-language walkthrough of exactly what the vehicle needs.

Cost to You

Zero. Estimates and second opinions are always free.

02 Claim

We Handle the Insurer

Paperwork, documentation, and adjuster negotiation all run through us. When an insurer pushes aftermarket parts or cut labor hours, that conversation happens with our shop, never with you.

You Provide

Claim number, keys, and a signature. That's the entire list.

03 Repair

OEM Repair, Scheduled Updates

100% OEM parts, factory weld and refinish procedures, and ADAS recalibration where the manufacturer requires it. You receive scheduled updates at every phase, so you always know where the vehicle stands and when you'll drive it home.

Parts Standard

Factory part numbers only. No aftermarket, ever.

04 Delivery

Delivered Under Lifetime Warranty

You get the vehicle back indistinguishable from factory, along with the complete repair file: parts invoices, measurements, and calibration reports. The work is warrantied for as long as you own it.

Coverage

Lifetime, for as long as you own the vehicle.
