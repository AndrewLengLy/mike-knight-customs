Repair Program

OEM-Approved Welders + SPR

Manufacturer Repair Spec

Aluminum does not repair like steel, and treating it like steel is how trucks get ruined. We join aluminum exclusively with manufacturer-approved welders and self-piercing rivet systems, following the OEM procedure for every panel. Much of that work ends up hidden under moldings and trim, and we hold it to the same standard as the paint you can see.
